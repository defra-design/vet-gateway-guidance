Animal by-products or products not for human consumption: Import Information Notes

Import Information Notes (IINs) contain statutory guidance for vets, Border Control staff and importers to use when importing live animals and animal products to Great Britain.

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Animal and Plant Health Agency

 

Contents

  1. General and clarifying information
  2. IINs for ABPs and products not for human consumption

These are the IINs for animal by-products (ABPs) and products not for human consumption only.

You can also find IINs for:

  • imports of live animals and genetic material
  • animal products for human consumption
  • vet checks and returned animals or products

General and clarifying information

IINs GEN/3 and GEN/4 must be read alongside the individual IINs for ABPs or products not for human consumption.
