Details

Veterinary surgeons can use this form to submit samples from animals, gametes and embryos to meet the testing requirements for an export health certificate.

Testing is carried out by the Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA).

You can use the same form for multiple types of test.

Check the export health certificates (EHC) to find out which tests need to be carried out.


Use the correct form

You can use this form to test most animal species, gametes and embryos for most export purposes.

Certain species and diseases require a different form.

Use a different form to test:

  • horses for the CEM organism for export and breeding
  • horses for West Nile virus: export, import, vaccination status and to exclude notifiable diseases
  • birds for viruses for export, import or quarantine
  • cattle for bovine genital campylobacter and trichomonas

Submitting samples

Package the samples according to the postal regulations for pathological material.

Fees, test codes and turnaround times

Search for the fees, codes and maximum turnaround times for each test on the APHA export price list.

Fast track service

You can select the APHA fast track service on the form.

With the APHA fast track service:

  • your sample testing is prioritised
  • samples are tested as soon as possible, either on the day of their arrival, or the day after, depending on delivery time and test protocol
  • results are sent the same day as the test is complete
  • costs £75.30 for every sample in each test

Some tests cannot be fast tracked. You will be contacted if this is the case and not be charged.

How to pay

You enter into a contract with APHA when the lab receives your sample and submission form.

An invoice will be sent to you with instructions on how to set up an account to make payment.

You can read the APHA general terms and conditions which apply to testing where no specific contract is signed.

Receiving your test results

Check the price list for the maximum processing time for each test.

Test results are returned as soon as possible. If you need the findings urgently, you should include this on the form.

Reports are sent by email. You can ask for the report to be sent by post. Reports sent by special delivery will result in extra charges and you will need to ask the centre for this service. You can also ask that results are sent to recipients other than yourself.

Confirm a test report’s validity

You can check that a previous test report was issued by APHA. You will need to provide a scan or photo of the report. Contact APHA Weybridge.
