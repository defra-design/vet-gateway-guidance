Import Information Notes

Import Information Notes (IINs) contain statutory guidance for vets, importers and border officials to use when importing live animals and animal products to Great Britain.

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Animal and Plant Health Agency

Import Information Notes (IINs) are technical documents containing import requirements. They are intended for use at Border Control Posts (BCPs) by vets, importers and border officials.

IINs provide a summary of general conditions and veterinary checks for imports of live animals and animal products into Great Britain.

They may not cover situations where emergency safeguard action has been taken to prohibit the importation of certain animals or products from countries following an outbreak of serious disease.
