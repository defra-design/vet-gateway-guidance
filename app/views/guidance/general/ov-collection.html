Official Veterinarians (OVs) and OV practices: forms and guidance

The processes for becoming an Official Veterinarian (OV), maintaining your authorisation, making claims, and what to do if you'll be TB testing.

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Animal and Plant Health Agency

 

Official Veterinarians (OVs) are veterinarians in private business who are authorised to deliver veterinary services such as TB testing, brucella and anthrax surveillance testing, export health certification and notifiable disease outbreak work.
