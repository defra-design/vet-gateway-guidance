ABPs or products not for human consumption: Import Information Notes

Import Information Notes (IINs) contain requirements for imports from outside the EU and from Member States of the EU, for use by veterinary staff at Border Control Posts.

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Animal and Plant Health Agency
