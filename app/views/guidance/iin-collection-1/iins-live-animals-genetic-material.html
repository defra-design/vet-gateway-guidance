Imports of live animals and genetic material: Import Information Notes

Import Information Notes (IINs) contain requirements for imports from outside the EU and from Member States of the EU, for use by veterinary staff at Border Control Posts.

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Animal and Plant Health Agency

 

Return to view all categories of IINs or find specific IINs for:

  • animal products for human consumption
  • animal by-products and products not for human consumption
  • vet checks and returned animals or products

General and clarifying information

IINs GEN/1 and GEN/5 must be read alongside the individual IINs for live animals and genetic material.
