Sam: register or sign in to submit TB test results

Use Sam to submit TB test results online if you are an Official Veterinarian (OV) or Approved TB Tester (ATT)

From: Animal Plant and Health Agency

Sam is APHA's electronic system for recording TB test submissions.

The practice that you test for must be registered on Sam in order for you to be able to submit your TB tests results.

How to register for Sam

You can register for Sam by completing the OV59 form and returning it to APHA's OV team. Your sign in details will then be sent to your practice address within 10 working days.

OVs and ATTs must finish their training before they can register for Sam.

All ATTs must register on Sam. ATT authorisation will not be granted until this is complete.

Planned downtime

Sam will be unavailable during the following periods:

  • 9am Sunday 17 March to 8am Monday 18 March
  • 7pm Tuesday 9 April to 8am Wednesday 10 April
  • 7pm Tuesday 14 May to 8am Wednesday 15 May
  • 9am Sunday 9 June to 8am Monday 10 June
  • 7pm Tuesday 11 June to 8am Wednesday 12 June
  • 9am Sunday 16 June to 8am Monday 17 June
  • 9am Sunday 8 September to 8am Monday 9 September
  • 9am Sunday 15 September to 8am Monday 16 September
  • 9am Sunday 8 December to 8am Monday 13 December

Recording data while Sam is unavailable

To record data while Sam is unavailable you can use:

  • the electronic (excel) TB52 form
  • a paper copy of the TB52 form
  • an excel XML file for use in third party software, such as TB Master

All data will need to be uploaded or manually entered into Sam when it is available again.

Guidance on how to access and use the TB52 form can be found in sections 10 and 12 of the Sam Official Veterinarian Practice Guide.

Support with using Sam

You can get support with accessing or using Sam by contacting the Sam support desk:

  • by phone on 03300 416999
  • by email at APHAServiceDesk@apha.gov.uk

If you have entered data into Sam incorrectly

To fix incorrect data in Sam, download and complete form GENO2 then email it to:

  • csc.TBsamdatafix@apha.gov.uk for CPHs in England
  • Apha.cymruwales@apha.gov.uk for CPHs in Wales
  • Apha.scotland@apha.gov.uk for CPHs in Scotland
