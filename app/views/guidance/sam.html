Use Sam to submit TB test results online if you are an Official Veterinarian (OV) or Approved TB Tester (ATT)

To use this service, you’ll need to prove your identity using Government Gateway. You’ll be able to register for Government Gateway if you have not used it before.

To record data while Sam is unavailable you can use:

  • the electronic (excel) TB52 form
  • a paper copy of the TB52 form
  • an excel XML file for use in third party software, such as TB Master

All data will need to be uploaded or manually entered into Sam when it is available again. Guidance on how to access and use the TB52 form can be found in sections 10 and 12 of the Sam Official Veterinarian Practice Guide.

How to enrol in Sam

All OVs and ATTs who will be carrying out TB testing must register for Government Gateway and enrol in Sam. ATT authorisation will not be granted until this is complete.

The practice that you test for must also enrol in Sam before you will be able to submit your TB tests results.

  1. Complete OV59 form and return it to APHA's OV team.
  2. Your Sam enrolment information (form AH046) will be sent to your practice address within 10 working days.
  3. Register for a Government Gateway account if you have not done so already.
  4. Sign in to Government Gateway and enter the Sam enrolment information.

Support with using Sam

You can get support with accessing or using Sam by contacting the Sam support desk:

  • by phone on 03300 416999
  • by email at APHAServiceDesk@apha.gov.uk

If you have entered data into Sam incorrectly

To fix incorrect data in Sam, download and complete form GENO2 then email it to:

  • csc.TBsamdatafix@apha.gov.uk for CPHs in England
  • Apha.cymruwales@apha.gov.uk for CPHs in Wales
  • Apha.scotland@apha.gov.uk for CPHs in Scotland
