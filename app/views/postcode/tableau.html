Post-mortem examination and carcase collection locations

Vets can use this service to find out:

  • if their location is eligible for free carcase collection
  • their nearest Animal and Plant Health Agecy (APHA) surveillance pathology centre

Use the postcode of the carcase's location and not a veterinary practice.

Enter the first part of the postcode only. For example, GU51.

About this service

APHA offers free carcase collection in many areas of England and Wales, where the location of the farm is outside the immediate catchment area of a surveillance pathology centre.

Use this service to find the nearest surveillance pathology centre to the farm and to find out if a carcase is eligible for free collection.

Read the detailed guidance on how to submit farm animals to APHA for a post-mortem examination.
